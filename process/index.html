How We'll Work Together

Most freelancers hand you a finished website and disappear. I don't work that way. Every project follows a clear, structured process designed to eliminate confusion and avoid surprises.

Here's exactly what to expect, from first conversation to launch.

A Clear, Transparent Project Flow

1

Strategy Call

Before we talk timelines or budgets, we figure out if we're a fit.

30 Minutes | Free

What We'll Cover

  • Your business goals & success metrics
  • Target audience & user actions
  • Current pain points & technical issues
  • Realistic timeline & expectations

What Happens Next

  • Good fit: I send a custom proposal
  • Not a fit: Honest feedback & referrals
  • No pressure, just clarity
2

Planning & Kickoff

We avoid missing info and bad assumptions by starting with structure.

Week 1

What I Need From You

  • Brand assets (logos, fonts, colors)
  • Content for key pages
  • Access to hosting/tools
  • Examples of sites you like

What You Get From Me

  • Sitemap & project blueprint
  • Wireframes for key pages
  • Tech stack recommendations
  • Clear timeline with milestones
3

Design & Development

The work happens here. You see progress as we go, not just at the end.

Weeks 2-6+

How This Works

  • Custom themes/code (no bloat)
  • Staging site for real-time progress
  • Performance baked in from start
  • Responsive & accessible focus

What To Expect

  • Weekly progress updates
  • Transparent communication
  • No surprises on scope/timing
  • Video calls for major decisions
4

Review & Testing

Ensuring functionality, performance, and compatibility across all devices.

Weeks 6-8

What Gets Tested

  • Forms, links & interactivity
  • PageSpeed scores (Target: 90+)
  • Browser & device compatibility
  • SEO foundations & schema

Your Review Process

  • Round 1: Full walkthrough
  • Round 2: Refinements
  • Round 3: Final Sign-off
  • Unlimited feedback phase
5

Launch

A careful, documented deployment to avoid downtime and errors.

Weeks 7-9

Launch Checklist

  • Full backup of previous site

  • Analytics setup verified
